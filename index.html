Significant Figures — Key Rules
1
All non-zero digits are significant.
1.234 g → 4 sig figs
2
Zeros between non-zero digits are always significant.
1002 g → 4 sig figs
3
Leading zeros (before the first non-zero digit) are NOT significant.
0.0025 g → 2 sig figs
4
Trailing zeros after a decimal point are significant.
2.500 g → 4 sig figs
5
In multiplication/division: answer has the same # of sig figs as the factor with the fewest sig figs.
12.011 × 6 = 72.066 → 72.1 (3 SF)
6
In addition/subtraction: round to the fewest decimal places.
12.011 + 15.999 = 28.010 g/mol
